On the road

Local Insider Notes

Small details that make each route feel different.

  1. 1

    Western coast / Maronite village

    Koruçam

    Visit village bakeries early in the day, as traditional bread and pastries often sell out before the afternoon.

  2. 2

    Kyrenia west / Coastal route

    Lapta

    Time the coastal walk for the hour before sunset, when the promenade is cooler and the Kyrenia mountains catch the last light.

  3. 3

    Kyrenia range / Mountain route

    Buffavento

    Start early and carry water; cloud and wind conditions can change quickly near the castle ridge.

  4. 4

    Eastern range / Panoramic route

    Kantara

    The clearest long-distance views usually appear after windy or rainy weather has cleared the air.

  5. 5

    Karpas gateway / Coastal drive

    Mehmetçik

    Combine the village and vineyard area with a late lunch near Boğaz harbour instead of rushing directly toward Bafra.
