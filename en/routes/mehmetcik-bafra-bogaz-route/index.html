Village, Vineyard & Coastal Route

Mehmetçik Vineyards, Bafra Coast & Boğaz Harbour

Travel from the village centre and vineyard landscapes of Mehmetçik toward the open Bafra coastline before finishing beside the fishing boats and restaurants of Boğaz Harbour.

Stops 5 locations
Duration Full day
Difficulty Moderate
Route type Village & coast

Route overview

From village life to the Karpas coastline

This route begins in Mehmetçik, where village life, seasonal events and vineyard landscapes reflect the agricultural character of the region. The journey then continues toward the wide coastline at Bafra before finishing at Boğaz Fishing Harbour, where small fishing boats and waterfront restaurants create a relaxed final stop.

Five selected stops

Route itinerary

1

Mehmetçik Village Centre

Begin in the centre of Mehmetçik and explore the village streets at a slow pace. Local businesses, traditional buildings and the surrounding rural landscape provide an introduction to daily life in the region.

2

Mehmetçik Festival Square

Continue to the square associated with local festivals and community events. The atmosphere changes throughout the year, but the area remains an important social point within the village.

3

Vineyard Landscape Stop

Drive through the agricultural roads around Mehmetçik and stop only at a safe roadside location. The vineyards and open fields show the relationship between the village, its landscape and local production.

4

Bafra Public Beach

Continue east toward Bafra and take a break beside the open coastline. The public beach provides sea views, swimming opportunities in suitable conditions and a quieter pause before the final drive toward Boğaz.

5

Boğaz Fishing Harbour

Finish beside the fishing boats and waterfront restaurants of Boğaz. The harbour is suitable for an evening walk, coastal photography and a relaxed meal after completing the route.

Before you drive

Practical information

Best time

Begin in Mehmetçik during the morning and reach Bafra during the afternoon before continuing to Boğaz for the evening.

Road conditions

Main roads are generally manageable, but some village and agricultural roads may be narrow or uneven. Avoid entering unmarked private tracks.

What to bring

  • Drinking water
  • Sun protection
  • Swimming equipment
  • A charged phone

Vineyard access

Vineyards and agricultural fields may be privately owned. View them from public roads unless clear permission is provided.

Beach conditions

Check the wind, waves and local safety conditions before swimming. Avoid leaving valuables visible inside the vehicle.

Food planning

Mehmetçik and Boğaz offer different food options, but opening times may change outside the main tourism season.

Recommendation

Spend enough time in Mehmetçik instead of treating it only as a road toward Bafra. Complete the village and vineyard stops first, pause at the beach during the afternoon and plan your final meal around Boğaz Harbour near sunset.
