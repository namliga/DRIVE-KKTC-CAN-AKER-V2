Eastern Castle & Coastal Route

Kantara Castle & East Coast Panorama

Travel from one of Northern Cyprus’s most dramatic mountain castles through forest roads, rural landscapes and the quieter eastern coastline.

Stops 5 locations
Duration Full day
Difficulty Moderate
Route type Castle & coast

Route overview

From a mountain fortress to the eastern coastline

This route begins at Kantara Castle, one of the strongest panoramic viewpoints in Northern Cyprus. From the castle, the journey continues through the nearby village and forest landscapes before descending toward Kaplıca and finishing with a cultural stop at Minia Kıbrıs.

Five selected stops

Route itinerary

1

Kantara Castle

Begin at the medieval castle positioned high above the eastern Kyrenia range. The ruins provide wide views across the northern coast, the Mesaoria plain and the surrounding mountain landscape.

2

Kantara Village

Continue into the nearby village for a quieter look at rural life in the eastern mountains. The settlement provides a natural pause after the castle visit and before entering the forest roads.

3

Mersinlik Forest

Drive through the forested landscape around Mersinlik, where pine-covered hills and winding roads create one of the route’s most peaceful sections. Stop only in safe areas away from traffic.

4

Kaplıca Coast

Descend from the mountain landscape toward the coast at Kaplıca. The area offers an open beach, sea views and a relaxed atmosphere after the higher and more demanding castle section.

5

Minia Kıbrıs

Finish at Minia Kıbrıs, where miniature models present important architectural and cultural landmarks from across Cyprus. It creates a lighter final stop after the castle, forest and coastal sections.

Before you drive

Practical information

Best time

Visit Kantara Castle in the morning for clearer views and cooler conditions, then continue toward the coast during the afternoon.

Road conditions

Mountain and forest roads can be narrow and winding. Drive slowly, particularly around blind corners and uneven roadside sections.

What to bring

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Drinking water
  • Sun and wind protection
  • A charged phone

Castle access

The castle contains uneven stone paths and exposed sections. Walk carefully and avoid climbing damaged walls or restricted structures.

Coastal planning

Sea conditions and wind can change quickly around Kaplıca. Check the coast before swimming and avoid isolated areas after dark.

Opening times

Confirm the opening hours of Minia Kıbrıs before beginning the route, especially outside the main tourism season.

Recommendation

Complete the castle visit before midday and avoid rushing directly to the coast. Take a short break in Kantara Village or Mersinlik Forest, then reach Kaplıca during the cooler late-afternoon hours.
